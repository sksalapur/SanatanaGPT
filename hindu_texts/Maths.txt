Important Discrete Mathematics Concepts:

1. Logic:
   - Propositional and Predicate Logic, Truth Tables, Logical Equivalence

2. Set Theory:
   - Union, Intersection, Complement, Power Sets

3. Graph Theory:
   - Graph types, Trees, Eulerian and Hamiltonian paths, Graph coloring

4. Combinatorics:
   - Permutations and Combinations, Pigeonhole Principle

5. Number Theory:
   - Prime numbers, GCD, Modular Arithmetic, Euclidean Algorithm
