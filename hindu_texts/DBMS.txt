Important DBMS Concepts:

1. Normalization:
   - 1NF, 2NF, 3NF, BCNF - Reduces data redundancy and anomalies

2. Transactions and Concurrency Control:
   - ACID Properties, Serializability, Locking protocols

3. SQL:
   - DDL, DML commands
   - Joins, Subqueries, Aggregation

4. Indexing:
   - B+ Trees, Hash Indexing for faster search

5. ER Model:
   - Entities, Relationships, Keys, Cardinality
